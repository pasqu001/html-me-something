Ada Lovelace:

Life: 1815-1852

She was a mathematician, chiefly known for her work on Charles Babbage's proposed mechanical general-purpose computer, the Analytical Engine. She was the first to recognise that the machine had applications beyond pure calculation, and published the first algorithm intended to be carried out by such a machine. She is considered one of the first computer programmers.
